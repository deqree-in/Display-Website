About Us

Deqree was founded on the belief that vital records should be able to be managed in a secure tamper-proof manner and should be easily verifiable. We started from the ground zero, rethinking how records were produced in India, how they could be made tamper-proof, and how they could be validated quickly and from anywhere free of cost while ensuring the privacy. We don't only want to make a platform where users can verify credentials; we also wanted to make the procedure as easy as possible so that universities, students and recruiters can use our tamper-proof digital documentation with a single click. We also plan to deploy smart-contracts for usecases under special circumstances with the help of a next generation peer-reviewed Blockchain (Cardano).
